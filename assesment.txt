Thinkly Labs - Software Engineering role.

What You're Building
A chatbot. Pick any topic you find interesting a character, a product, a niche subject, anything and build a chatbot trained on that topic. The bot should feel purpose-built for that subject, not like a generic chat wrapper.
The experience you design around it is the actual assignment.
Requirements
The Product
Chatbot trained on a topic of your choice (you decide the knowledge base, the scope)
The UI should reflect the topic and usability of the product; this is where your frontend thinking shows
Functional, responsive, and deployed on Vercel or of your choice
What "Frontend Thinking" Means Here
How do you present the chatbot? What's the first thing a user sees?
How does the conversation feel when loading states, error states, empty states?
What small details did you add that show you thought about the user?
AI Usage
Use AI tools however you want, Cursor, v0, Claude, Copilot, all of it. The goal is to understand how well you can direct AI and make things work.
What to Submit
Deployed Vercel link must be live and working
GitHub repo public, with a short README explaining what you built and why you picked the topic
A short Loom (5–10 min) walk us through your coding session, we want to understand how you prompt, check code and ensure no ai slop.
